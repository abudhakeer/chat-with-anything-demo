Acme Analytics — Q1 2026 Summary (Sample Document)

Revenue grew 18% year-over-year to $4.2M, driven primarily by enterprise renewals
in healthcare and logistics. Gross margin improved from 61% to 64% after migrating
inference workloads to edge infrastructure.

Key risks called out by leadership:
1. Customer concentration — top three accounts represent 42% of ARR.
2. Regulatory review in the EU market may delay two pending deals until Q3.
3. Hiring lag in customer success; NPS slipped from 58 to 51.

Product highlights:
- Document chat latency dropped from 8s to under 2s median after switching to
  managed retrieval instead of a custom embedding pipeline.
- Vision Q&A for uploaded charts reached 89% helpfulness in pilot feedback.
